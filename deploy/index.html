About Us and Coffee Alchemy

Peddler Coffee opened in 2009 in the great city of Philadelphia. We are located in the heart of the city right behind the Franklin Institute. We wanted to do more than just sell coffee. We wanted to sell an alchemy of coffee. With our custom made coffee bags you can take the freshness and raw quality of Peddler Coffee right to your kitchen.

With a variety of coffee flavors its easy to find which coffee fits you and your mornings, or afternoons. Being in the city of brotherly Love gives us the chance to spread that same love and positivity through our fresh ground coffee and engagement with the community. We are always active with our daily customers, especially on social media. Next time you have a Peddler Coffee dont be afraid to tag and mention us in you stories/ posts!

Email us with any reviews or concerns you have. Peddler Coffee is a local coffee shop and would love to create more connections with those in the community!
